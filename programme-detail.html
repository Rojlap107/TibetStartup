Tibetan Enterprise Digitization Support Program (TEDSP)

Thursday, May 15, 2025, 10:30 PM – Wednesday, December 31, 2025, 11:30 PM

It all begins with an idea - maybe you want to launch a business, turn a hobby into something more, or share a creative project with the world. Whatever it is, the way you tell your story online can make all the difference.

The Tibetan Enterprise Digitization Support Program (TEDSP) provides a comprehensive pathway for Tibetan businesses

to transition to the digital realm. The program focuses on foundational support, including legal registrations (e.g., income tax and GST compliance), creating an online presence through websites and social media, and developing a marketing plan. Businesses will also receive guidance on promoting their products, generating sales, and utilizing digital marketing tools effectively.

Program Services Include:

  • Legal Registration
  • Payment Gateway Setup
  • Branding & Rebranding
  • Social Media Marketing
  • Website Development
  • Digital Marketing Strategy
  • E-commerce Integration
  • Business Analytics Setup
  • Ongoing Support & Mentorship
  • Performance Tracking & Optimization

TibetStartup! is the non-profit arm of Norzin Consultancy, dedicated to empowering Tibetan entrepreneurs and strengthening the Tibetan start-up ecosystem. Its vision is rooted in fostering economic self-reliance within the Tibetan community, recognizing that a thriving entrepreneurial landscape is vital for the sustainability of the Tibetan movement.
